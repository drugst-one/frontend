Network Exploration and Drug Repurposing on your website


Drugst.One turns your tool that outputs a list of genes or proteins into a feature rich, drug repurposing web tool with interactive network visualization.

With just 3 lines of code, users of your website can benefit from the exploratory functions and immediately start researching the results of your tool.

See how easily Drugst.One is integrated to serve as a result visualization of a simple HTML website in this tutorial video.
Check out the options and resources that let you interactively configure your drugst.one network, features and style.
Drugst.One can be used by redirecting to a standalone implementation and freely configured to upgrade any tool.
